## 📖 About The Project

VotingSystem provides a way to cast votes through your computer. It uses blockchain technology to record each vote. This process makes the election results permanent and impossible to change. The system uses your webcam to verify your identity. This prevents fraud. You receive a digital record of your vote once you finish the process.

## 🗳️ Casting Your Vote

The voting process involves three simple phases.

### Phase 1: Identity Verification
The software asks you to look into your webcam. The system compares your current image with your registered profile. This ensures that only authorized voters access the digital ballot. 

### Phase 2: Selecting Candidates
A list of available candidates appears on your screen. You select your choice by clicking the round button next to their name. You can review your choice before you finalize the process.

### Phase 3: Voting On The Blockchain
The application sends your choice to the blockchain network. This creates a permanent, digital record. The ledger confirms your vote has been recorded successfully. You see a confirmation message on your screen when the transaction finishes.

## 🛡️ Privacy And Security

Security remains the purpose of this software. Traditional election systems involve paper ballots that can be lost or altered. Our system uses a distributed network. No single person or group manages the ledger. The blockchain structure prevents anyone from deleting or modifying previous votes. Your identity remains protected through encryption. The software destroys local copies of your facial data once it converts them into a secure digital key.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I vote more than once?**
No. The blockchain records your individual signature. The system rejects any attempts to cast a second vote from the same user account.

**Is my identity public?**
No. Your personal details remain local. The blockchain only stores a hash, which acts as a mathematical finger print. No one can link a vote back to your specific identity.

**What happens if the internet cuts out?**
The system pauses the transaction. It resumes the recording process once the connection returns. It ensures you never lose a vote due to network errors.

**Who runs the blockchain?**
A network of independent computers verifies every transaction. This decentralized approach ensures transparency and fairness.

**How do I update the software?**
The software checks for updates automatically when it starts. It prompts you to install them if a newer version exists.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, try these steps:

*   **Camera not working:** Check if other programs are using your camera and close them. Restart the application.
*   **Slow performance:** Close unnecessary background applications to free up memory.
*   **Connection error:** Ensure your internet connection functions properly. Disable any VPN software if you face issues connecting to the network.
*   **Registration failure:** Ensure you are in a well-lit area. Clear your camera lens of dust or smudges.
